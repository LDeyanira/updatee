Mision

Train highly human resources to carry out Research activities, project development and leaders in applied engineering with the analytical capacity to design, propose, develop, innovate and process various types of sustainable materials to contribute to the technological and scientific development of the region, the state of Hidalgo and the country.

Vision

Generate basic and applied Knowledge in the area of engineering and design of sustainable systems with high added value and its transfer to the social, academic, and productive sectors at the local, national and international level.

Lines of Generation and Aplication Knowledge

Development of systems under the synergy of mechatronics and optics focused on diverse applications: diagnosis and treatment of diseases, image processing, quality control, energy, synthesis and characterization of materials, environmental, industrial or other multidisciplinary nature. Likewise, the processing of large volumes of data as support for these applications.
